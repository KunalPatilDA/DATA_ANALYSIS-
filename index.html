What a Data Analyst Does
📊
Build Reports & Dashboards
Design automated, interactive reports in Power BI, Tableau, or Excel that help stakeholders track KPIs in real time.
🔍
Find Trends in Data
Identify patterns, seasonality, and anomalies in large datasets using SQL, Python, and statistical methods.
🧹
Clean & Prepare Data
Transform raw, messy data into reliable, analysis-ready datasets using Pandas, SQL, and Power Query.
💡
Generate Business Insights
Translate numbers into clear stories and actionable recommendations that drive business decisions.
🤝
Collaborate with Teams
Work alongside Product, Marketing, Finance, and Operations teams to answer key business questions with data.
📋
Document & Present
Present data findings through clear reports, slide decks, and dashboards for both technical and non-technical audiences.
